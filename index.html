    Message by Head of the Department

    Dr. A Vani Vathsala (Professor and HOD )

    The department of Computer Science and Engineering has been flourishing by leaps and bounds since its inception in 2001 and has seen unprecedented growth. It currently offers four undergraduate program B.Tech CSE and a postgraduate program M.Tech Artificial Intelligence. The department is a JNTUH recognised Research Centre. B.Tech CSE program has been accredited four times by NBA from 2007 onwards. The fourth accreditation is under Tier-1 from July 2019 for a period of three years. CSE Department has well-equipped computing facilities including Apple Systems, NVIDIA GPUs, IBM Servers, Raspberry pi, Intel multi core systems. It has 37 Lab units of 30 Systems each, with the latest open source software tools needed for B.Tech and M.Tech Curriculum. Oracle 10g, Eclipse, Open XML, Star UML, My SQL, Anaconda, Weka, Android Studio, Visual Studio Code, Angular and React Libraries, Node JS, to name a few.
