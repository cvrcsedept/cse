Class Rooms

The class rooms are spacious and creates an atmosphere to specialize the students to acquire education through well-equipped class rooms. Our classrooms are equipped with green chalk boards, slide projectors, and screens. Green chalk boards are used for classroom teaching. Projectors and screens are used to involve students in learning with technology. Notes, flowcharts, drawings and PPTs used in the course of teaching are saved to be accessed as and when required. Each classroom provides an occupancy of 60+ students.
